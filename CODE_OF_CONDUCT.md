# Code of Conduct

This project is a technical collaboration space for quantitative research, backtesting, simulation, and trading infrastructure. Keep discussion professional, evidence-based, and welcoming to contributors with different levels of market, Python, frontend, and operations experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, reviews, and discussions.
- Critique code, assumptions, tests, and documentation rather than people.
- Explain tradeoffs and reproducible evidence when disagreeing.
- Avoid sharing private credentials, proprietary broker materials, or personal account information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to provide investment advice.
- Encouraging unsafe live trading or bypassing broker, exchange, or compliance controls.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; or restrict participation when behavior disrupts the project or creates safety risk. If you see a problem, contact the maintainers with links and context.
